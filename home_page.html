PHILOSOPHY

At levels we seek to work in a unique way. We value our customers and consider their aspirations in every decision we make. We aim to serve you with integrity, devotion and dependability.

Our portfolio is diverse. It includes many types of buildings that vary between residential units, hotels, commercial buildings and administrative buildings. We also have the flexibility and the experience to take in more types of projects.

About us

Mission >> Our goal is to develop a unique new pattern of buildings. Buildings erected on outstanding luxurious designs with very high standards and suitable affordability and we are dedicated to our primary goal.

Background >> Levels was initially found by Architect Ahmed Nabil .It is located at Alexandria, Egypt. Levels also offers you an online service. We can serve you wherever you are either in Egypt or Saudi Arabia .Our concern is to please you because our customers are the core of every aspect in our development.

The team>> In levels we work and cooperate with each other to ensure commitment to our customers .We accomplish our projects to the finest detail flawlessly. We make your interests our priority. Our staff includes architects, interior designers, civil engineers, mechanical engineers and electrical engineers. We have been working as a team since almost 4 years and have been experienced working in Egypt and Gulf countries (coordinating - arranging).Regardless of the different approaches and roles we have, we work as a team to achieve our main goal.

في ليفلز ستوديو نعمل لتحقيق امنيات عملائنا في الحصول على المسكن الذى لطالما حلموا به، نستطيع ايصال احلامكم الى افكار على الورق ثم تحويلها الى واقع . نحن لا نبنى فقط وحدات سكنية ، نحن نبنى بيوتاً .

Architecture Visualization and Rendering:

A lot count on architecture visualization because presenting an architecture model or an architecture view of a model assists with the concept , It lets us know if the theoretical principles of the concept can be applied practically and we have the skills to render and practically apply all of our designs .
